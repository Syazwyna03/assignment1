Step by Step Guide to Become a Lawyer

  1. Complete SPM with excellent results
  2. Take STPM or a Foundation in Law programme
  3. Obtain a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) degree
  4. Pass the Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP) or Bar Exam
  5. Undergo chambering (pupillage) for 9 months
  6. Get admitted to the Bar and become a practicing lawyer
  7. Gain experience and specialize in a legal field
