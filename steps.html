Step-by-Step Guide to Becoming a Lawyer

Home Guide Inspiration
  1. Complete SPM with excellent results
  2. Take Pre-Law or Foundation in Law
  3. Obtain a Law Degree (LLB)
  4. Complete Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP) or Bar
  5. Undergo pupilage (chambering)
  6. Pass Bar Exam or CLP exam
  7. Get called to the Bar and begin practice
