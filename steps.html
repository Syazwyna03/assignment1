Steps to Become a Lawyer

  1. Finish secondary school with strong results
  2. Enroll in a recognized law program at a university
  3. Complete a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree
  4. Attend professional legal training or chambering
  5. Pass the Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP) or equivalent
  6. Register with the Bar Council and begin practicing
